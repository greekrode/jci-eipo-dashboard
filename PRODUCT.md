# Product: IDX e-IPO Analytics

## What it is
A single-page analytics dashboard over a static census of Indonesian (IDX) IPOs, 2021–2026 (246 deals, 237 listed). It turns a 33-column spreadsheet into legible signal: day-1 to day-7 return behavior, underwriter performance (lead vs syndicate member), and sector / time trends.

## Who uses it
Roderick (builder of alpha-flow) and, by extension, numerate retail investors. Users are in a task: scanning distributions, comparing underwriters, looking up a specific IPO. Their reference points are Bloomberg, Stripe's dashboard, Linear.

## Register
Product. Design serves the task: information-dense, restrained, fast. The tool disappears into the data.

## Design direction
Semi-brutalist, concrete-dark. Flat near-black surfaces delineated by strong structural borders; near-square corners; a single blue accent; semantic green / red for returns. Inter for headings and prose, JetBrains Mono for all data, tickers, and labels (terminal feel). Boxed segmented navigation, dense mono data tables, square-edged charts, and seamless tab reveals. No gradients, glows, glassmorphism, emoji, or side-stripe accents.

## Core statistics
Median is the headline (returns are right-skewed); mean sits beside it, never alone. Dispersion (standard deviation), minimum, and maximum are reported. Return milestones at D1, D3, D5, D7. Every cut shows sample size n.
